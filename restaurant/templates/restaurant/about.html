A Taste of Lisbon

A traditional Portuguese restaurant in the heart of London.

Our menus at A Taste of Lisbon display the delicious cuisine of Portugal and celebrate its international influence from its naviagation history. We cook Portuguese traditional dishes as well as some modern twists on classic recipes. We want to celebrate the diversity and interconnection of Portuguese food and culture. A Taste of Lisbon is the culmination of lifelong experimentation and discovery. Our heritage dates back to before the 16th century and the passion for cooking has been predominant in our family for many generations. What we discovered is delicous and beautiful cuisine and the art of fine dining. Come join us and share with us a taste of home.
